"So let it be written, so let it be established – Esther 8"

From Governor Faith Sekhu – Kingdom Queens Mentorship Program
"The Awakening of a Kingdom Queen"

Presentation: The Royal Decree of Redemption (Esther Chapter 8)
Testimony: The Awakening of My Kingdom Identity

When I behold Esther Chapter 8, I do not merely see history; I see a divine constitution for how heaven partners with earth. Esther, crowned as queen, discovered that true queenship is not in ornament, but in intercession. She became the womb of a nation's redemption.

Through the Kingdom Queens Mentorship Program and the 72 Hours of Consecration, God has awakened me to the reality of who I am in His Kingdom. I encountered the Spirit of the God-Head — the fullness of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and I realized that heaven resides within me. I am a carrier of divine essence, a vessel through which God's purposes manifest on earth.

Intercession is not passive pleading; it is legislative authority. Esther knew the royal seat was given to her for kingdom purpose. When the decree of death was issued, she did not remain silent. She arose in revelation, understood her redemptive identity, and birthed a decree that realigned history.

As I embraced the power of Kingdom identity, I discovered that true royalty is not about crowns or titles, but about alignment and obedience. My identity is my throne, a place of divine authority where I legislate heaven's will through prayer and decree.

I now understand that the kingdom of God is within me. Often it lies dormant until I awaken to my identity. Esther 8 awakens me to my own redemptive mandate — that I am a vessel, a womb through which God can birth decrees that reconfigure nations. Influence without intercession is impotent. True power flows when life aligns with the Word of God, sealed by the blood of Jesus.

The Spirit of Wisdom taught me to operate on thrones, not through human intellect but divine revelation. The Spirit of Wisdom is my compass, guiding me to govern through discernment and intimacy with the King.

God, though sovereign, seeks partnership. He has entrusted authority to mankind and awaits a generation that will steward the womb of identity and release decrees that birth His purposes. Esther 8 is not just history; it is my call today.

When I encountered the Mystery of the Womb, I realized that within me is a spiritual womb, the creative chamber where heaven's purposes are conceived. My prayers and intercessions became contractions that birth God's will for nations. My life is a womb of transformation through which destinies are birthed.

I rise as a kingdom queen, set apart to use influence for victory, not vanity; for purpose, not position. I posture my life as a vessel through which heaven invades the structures of nations. My obedience and intercession will birth decrees of life, healing, and restoration over families, communities, and nations.

Then came the revelation of Esther Chapter 8; a divine mirror that reflected my calling. Like Esther, I was reminded that influence without intercession is powerless.

God positioned me not for vanity, but for victory. I am called to stand in the gap, to legislate in prayer, and to rewrite decrees over families, communities, and nations.

By the authority of the King of Kings, I decree: let every decree of death over my life, family, and nation be revoked. Let the scrolls of heaven open and new decrees of life and divine alignment be written and sealed in the blood of Jesus. I rise as Esther rose. I legislate as Esther legislated. So let it be written, so let it be established, in Jesus' mighty Name. Amen.

During the 72 Hours of Consecration, I felt heaven's refining fire. I laid down my will and picked up His. My time in prayer became a birthing room where decrees of life replaced decrees of death. I came out renewed, repositioned, and reanointed.

Today, I testify that I am a Kingdom Queen crowned with revelation, robed in intercession, and anointed to birth God's purposes on earth. Every decree of death is revoked, and new decrees of life, restoration, and destiny are now established in Jesus' mighty Name. So let it be written, so let it be established. Amen.
