About Us

AWAKE, MY GLORY, AWAKE... PSALM 57:8

VISION
Awakening the Queen in every Woman and Restoring the Glory of God for Generational Transformation.

MISSION
To Prepare and Mentor Women and Girls for the Thrones. To Awake the Glory of Ladies' Spiritual Wombs for the Healing of the Nations. To Ignite the Power of Ladies' Kingdom Identity to carry God's Glory. To Empower and Equip the unemployed, broken and hopeless Women to Fulfill their Kingdom Purposes. To Restore Women's Faulty Foundations - Personal, Spiritual, Family and Marriage to Manifest God's Glory.
